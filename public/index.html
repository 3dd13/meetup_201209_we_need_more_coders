Problem

Let's start with the problem, and then we discuss causes and I will suggest some solutions. With no doubt the situation is getting worse. How long it took you to recruit a new coder ? How many ppl you talked to before you found the right guy ?

4 Years Ago,

We were looking for the BEST coders.

10 Years Experience in Ruby, knows frontend backend

3 Years Ago,

We looked for SMART coders.

Less experience is fine, if he is smart and keep track of web and latest technology

2 Years Ago,

We looked for PASSIONATE coders.

If he loves programming, then it's fine. we can teach him.

Last year ...

We looked for Java Engineers.

^___^ just kidding ... we should consider C/C++, Cobol and RPG engineers too.

Recently ...

We looked for ANY graduate students.

What will happen ?

Can't you see this problem ? such a serious problem ?

Problem IS

No one can find the right candidates.


Create them.
